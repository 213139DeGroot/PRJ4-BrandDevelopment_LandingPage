Who are we?

Our mission

Our mission is to make life easier for expats who are starting a new chapter in the Netherlands. We want to help them adjust to their new surroundings and feel like they belong here. We know how overwhelming it can be to move to a new country and we want to make the transition as smooth as possible. Our goal is to provide expats with the tools they need to feel confident and comfortable in their new home. We want to help them build connections and friendships in the Dutch community and create a support system that makes them feel at home.

Our vision

At Join-In, we recognize the constantly evolving nature of our world and understand that harmony and balance can only be achieved through collective care and support. We believe that by fostering a sense of community and encouraging empathy, we can create a world where individuals feel valued and supported, and where collaboration and cooperation are the driving forces for positive change.

Our values

Inclusivity

Given that our target audience is female expats, it's important for us to create an inclusive environment that welcomes everyone regardless of their background, culture, or identity. By embracing inclusivity as a core value, we can ensure that everyone feels valued and respected within our community

Community

Starting a new life in a foreign country can be daunting, so having a supportive community can make all the difference. By prioritizing community as a value, we can help create a sense of belonging among our audience, foster connections, and encourage people to support and help each other

Empowerment

Moving to a new country can also come with challenges, and we want to help our audience feel empowered to overcome them. By emphasizing empowerment as a value, we can inspire people to take control of their own lives, make informed decisions, and develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in their new environment.

Authenticity

In a world where social media can often promote unrealistic or unattainable lifestyles, we believe in being authentic and transparent. By valuing authenticity, we can create a more genuine and trustworthy brand, build stronger relationships with our audience, and promote a more positive and realistic view of expat life.
